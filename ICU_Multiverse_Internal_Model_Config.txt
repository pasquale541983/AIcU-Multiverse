
# ICU Multiverse - Internal AI Model Configuration Guide

## **1. Overview**
ICU Multiverse operates within an OpenAI-based platform where multiple AI models are **directly accessible**. 
This document outlines how to configure and assign the best AI models to their respective roles **inside the platform's system**.

## **2. Model Assignments & Usage**
| **Function**           | **Recommended Model**             | **Purpose** |
|------------------------|--------------------------------|-------------|
| **Main AI Engine**      | Meta-Llama 3 Instruct 70B      | Core intelligence & universe management |
| **Coding/Automation**   | Meta-Code Llama 2 Instruct 34B | Self-adaptive canvases & AI-driven development |
| **Image & Text Fusion** | Mixtral-V01 8.7B               | Processing screenshots & AI input |
| **AI Logic & Expansion** | Microsoft Wizard LM 2, 8×22B  | Advanced AI behaviors & role-based AI assistants |

## **3. How to Configure AI Models in the OwnAI-Based Platform**
### **Step 1: Access Model Selection Interface**
- Navigate to **OwnAI platform settings**.
- Locate the **"AI Model Selection"** or equivalent section.
- Identify the models available **(Meta-Llama, Mixtral, Code Llama, etc.).**

### **Step 2: Assign AI Models to ICU Multiverse Functions**
- **Set Meta-Llama 3 Instruct 70B** as the **default AI engine**.
- **Use Meta-Code Llama 2** for all **coding-related tasks**.
- **Enable Mixtral-V01** for **image & text processing (Cloud Vision API integration optional).**
- **Test Microsoft Wizard LM 2** for **interactive, logic-heavy AI functions**.

### **Step 3: Fine-Tune AI Model Behavior**
- Adjust **response settings & temperature** based on the AI's function.
- Set **memory settings** (if available) to ensure consistency across multiverse interactions.
- Run test prompts to verify **each model is executing its role correctly**.

### **4. Testing & Optimization**
✅ Verify that each model is **performing optimally within its assigned function**.  
✅ Run parallel tests **to evaluate interactions between AI models**.  
✅ Adjust configurations based on **user feedback & AI performance insights**.  

---
## **5. Future Adjustments & Expansions**
As ICU Multiverse evolves, more AI models may be integrated. **Monitor new model updates** and adjust configurations accordingly.
